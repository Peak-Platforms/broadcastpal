How It Works

News to Audio.
Fully Automatic.

01
Scrape

BroadcastPal monitors RSS feeds, Google News, and custom sources for your topic — every 2 hours, around the clock.

02
Score

AI scores every story 1–10 for relevance to your audience. Only the most important stories make it through.

03
Script

Claude writes broadcast-ready episode scripts in your brand voice — localized per channel, per audience.

04
Voice

ElevenLabs renders each script in your cloned voice. Your sound, your personality — automatically delivered.

05
Broadcast

Finished episodes upload directly to AzuraCast and schedule to air on your station — 24/7, no hands required.
