Welcome to BroadcastPal. This guide gets you live in under 10 minutes on game day.

1Your two links

Save these now. Share the viewer link with fans before kickoff.

Fans watch here
broadcastpal.fun/live
You direct the broadcast here
broadcastpal.fun/director
Share the viewer link in your group chat, on social media, or embed it on your booster club website. Anyone with the link can watch for free.

2What you need on game day

  • AMentra Live glasses — charged and paired to your phone via the MentraOS app
  • BA phone with the MentraOS app installed and connected to the glasses
  • CWiFi or a strong cell signal at the venue
  • DA laptop or tablet open to broadcastpal.fun/director for the director

3Going live — glasses operator

The person wearing the glasses does this:

  • 1Put on your Mentra Live glasses
  • 2Open the MentraOS app on your phone — confirm glasses are connected
  • 3Open BroadcastPal from the MiniApp list
  • 4You'll see: "📡 BroadcastPal — Say go live to start streaming"
  • 5Say the magic words:
"go live"

The display changes to 🔴 LIVE — you are now broadcasting your point of view to everyone watching at broadcastpal.fun/live.

Up to 4 people can stream simultaneously from different sections of the stadium. Each stream appears as a separate feed that viewers can choose from.

To stop streaming at any time:

"stop"

4Director — composing the broadcast

While glasses operators are live, the director opens broadcastpal.fun/director on a laptop.

  • 1Active streams appear as cards on the left
  • 2Tap the streams you want to include
  • 3Click Start Multiview
  • 4The composed broadcast appears at broadcastpal.fun/live under Multiview

The director can switch which fan's microphone is live at any time using the Audio Source buttons — useful when different fans are in better spots during the game.

5What fans experience

  • 👁Choose their own camera angle — any active POV feed or the composed multiview
  • 📻Listen to live radio alongside the video
  • 📊See the live score overlay burned into the broadcast automatically
  • 🔄Switch between games — OU, OSU, and Texas all in one place

6After the game

  • 1Glasses operators say "stop"
  • 2Director clicks Stop on any running sessions in the dashboard
  • 3Done — no other cleanup needed
